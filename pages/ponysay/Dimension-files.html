7.5 Dimension files

Pony dimension files are used by ponysay to determine the size of all ponies and use that information to determine which ponies fit the terminal and may be randomly selected.

Running ponysay-tool --dimensions PONY-DIR will generate three files widths, heights and onlyheights to the directory PONY-DIR, the contain optimised information about the widths, heigths and heights with printed without the balloon, respectively, for each pony the the directory. For use by the installer, the files to include can be explicity declared appending their basename to the command.
