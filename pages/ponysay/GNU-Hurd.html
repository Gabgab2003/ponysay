8.2 GNU Hurd

ponysay should work just fine on GNU/Hurd, except for in the native virtual terminal (TTY). Hurd’s terminal is limited to 16 colours and does not provide the capaility of modifing

If we are lucky it may be possible draw pictures, in full resultions, as you can in linux; which is currently not inplementedin ponysay. Another, not yet implemented possiblity, is to use low resoltion with ACSII character for colour interpolation.
